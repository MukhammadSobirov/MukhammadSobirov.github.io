Night Life in Istanbul

Istanbul is a city that never dies. This situation brings lots of alternatives to discover when the sun goes down. So, here is the most popular clubs, events and event halls in Istanbul.

Sortie

Sortie, which is located right next to the Bosphorus in Kuruçeşme and consists of 6 restaurants and 3 bars, is among the popular clubs of Istanbul with its magnificent the Bosphorus view, elite ambience, quality food and drink, music and entertainment. Sortie Club is open from 18:00 in the evening until 04:00 in the morning.

Ruby

Ruby is located in a fiery and sparkling location overlooking the sea in Ortaköy. Ruby offers a brand new entertainment experience in Istanbul with its magnificent the Bosphorus view and delicious menus. After dinner, you can experience unforgettable nights with dance and music in Ruby, either downstairs or upstairs, in the club sections until the first light of the morning. Ruby opens every day at 5 pm.

Last updated 3 mins ago

360Istanbul

360Istanbul is located located on the top floor of the historical Egyptian Passage in Istiklal Street. 360istanbul serves lunch and dinner in world and modern Turkish cuisine with impressive Istanbul goods, outstanding wine and a rich bar menu. 360 Istanbul, which has many international awards and hosts famous artists and DJs from abroad, turns into a Club with its live shows on weekends.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Nardis Jazz Club

Nardis is located close to Galata Tower. Nardis is a real jazz club and a place where jazz-oriented creative music is featured, where music and musicians stand out, and a concert environment where every performance is experienced in the comfort of a club and is a frequent destination for jazz lovers. Live jazz is offered at Nardis between 21.30-00.30 on weekdays and 22.30-01.30 on weekends.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Maestro Donizetti Jazz Club

Maestro Donizetti Jazz Club, named after the famous Italian conductor Giuseppe Donizetti, the club is located on the ground floor of the Palazzo Donizetti Hotel in the heart of Pera in Asmalımescit, the club welcomes its guests as one of Istanbul's new and popular jazz clubs. The club also aims to host masters as well as young people on its stage. You can have a pleasant Istanbul night with live music performances offered every week at Maestro Donizetti Jazz Club.
